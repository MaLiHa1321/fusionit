FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the benefits of enterprise software?

The adoption of suitable solutions can make a great difference to corporate efficiency and employee productivity. These tools can streamline cumbersome day-to-day tasks or whole workflows, saving employees’ time and the company’s money. Digitalizing corporate processes with software can also make them more transparent, traceable, and standardized. What is more, these solutions are a great source of relevant customer, employee, and efficiency information. Last but not least, digital enterprise tools make processes much more scalable, helping the adopters ensure consistent product or service quality in the long run.

How to choose between custom and off-the-shelf enterprise solutions?

Custom enterprise systems can be tailored to fully meet your unique needs and align with workflows, helping you to achieve your business goals faster. Platform-based solutions, on the other hand, are more generic and need to be properly configured or even modified with code to bring the expected value. On the other hand, bespoke tools take a significant amount of time and investment to develop and maintain down the line, compared to a typical licence fee and implementation cost of a platform-based solution. This is why the choice between custom enterprise software development and platform implementation is always individual.

What enterprise apps do you work with?

Fusion IT Solution has expertise in bespoke development or platform implementation of a wide variety of digital solutions. We are well-equipped to handle ERPs, CRMs, accounting and finance systems, HR solutions, supply chain management software, apps for document and content management, and process automation and collaboration tools. Our team is also equally well-versed in industry-specific apps, such as ecommerce portals and marketplaces, learning content management systems, core banking systems and additional tools, healthcare CRM, EHR/EMR, and hospital management systems, and many other solutions across domains.
